# Support Guide

**StayReel**  
**Effective Date: March 25, 2026**

---

## 1. Start Here

If something is not working, most issues can be fixed with one of these steps:

1. Fully close and reopen StayReel.
2. Make sure you are connected to the internet.
3. Update StayReel to the latest version.
4. If the issue involves Instagram, open Instagram directly on your phone first and confirm your account is fully logged in.
5. Try the action again once.

If the problem is related to rate limits or Instagram security checks, repeatedly retrying usually makes it worse. In those cases, wait first.

---

## 2. Account and Sign-In

### Problem: I got signed out of StayReel
This is your StayReel account session, not your Instagram account.

### Fix
1. Open StayReel.
2. Sign in again using your email.
3. Your saved data should still be there after you log back in.

### Problem: I cannot access my StayReel account
### Fix
1. Make sure you are using the same email address you originally used.
2. Check your email inbox and spam folder for sign-in messages.
3. Try signing in again after a few minutes.

---

## 3. Connecting Instagram

### Problem: Instagram will not connect
### Fix
1. Open Instagram on your phone.
2. Make sure you are fully logged in.
3. Return to StayReel and reconnect your Instagram account.
4. When connecting, log in all the way to your Instagram home feed before tapping Connect.

### Problem: "Session expired" or "Invalid session"
### Fix
1. In StayReel, go to **Settings → Disconnect Instagram**.
2. Open Instagram on your phone and log in normally.
3. Return to StayReel and reconnect your account.
4. If needed, scroll Instagram briefly before tapping Connect again.

### Problem: Instagram asks for verification or shows a challenge
This is an Instagram security check.

### Fix
1. Open the Instagram app directly.
2. Complete any security prompt, code check, or "Was this you?" verification.
3. Wait about 15 minutes.
4. Return to StayReel and reconnect Instagram.

### Problem: I disconnected Instagram by mistake
### Fix
1. Go to **Settings**.
2. Reconnect your Instagram account.
3. Your snapshot history should remain available unless you deleted your StayReel data.

---

## 4. Snapshots and Refreshes

### Problem: My snapshot will not start
### Fix
1. Confirm your Instagram account is still connected.
2. Confirm you can open Instagram normally on your phone.
3. Close and reopen StayReel.
4. Try again once.

### Problem: I see "Rate limited" or "Too many requests"
Instagram has temporarily slowed access to your account.

### Fix
1. Stop retrying.
2. Wait between 1 and 6 hours.
3. Try again later.

### Problem: A refresh is taking a long time
Large accounts can take longer to process.

### Fix
1. Keep the app open if possible.
2. Give the refresh time to finish.
3. If it fails, wait a few minutes before trying again.

### Problem: My follower numbers do not look perfectly real-time
StayReel works from snapshots captured at specific times.

### What to expect
- Counts and changes reflect the latest successful snapshot.
- If people follow or unfollow you after that snapshot, the app will not show those changes until the next one.

## 5. Lists, Locked Features, and Pro Access

### Problem: I can take snapshots, but some lists are locked
This is expected on the Free plan.

### What to know
- Snapshots are available on Free.
- Pro unlocks full follower-list visibility and paid features.

### Fix
1. Go to **Settings → Upgrade to Pro** if you want access.
2. If you already purchased Pro, use **Settings → Restore Purchases**.

---

## 6. Subscriptions and Purchases

### Problem: I paid, but Pro is not showing up
### Fix
1. Open StayReel.
2. Go to **Settings → Restore Purchases**.
3. Make sure you are signed into the same Apple ID or app-store account used for the purchase.
4. Close and reopen the app after restoring if needed.

### Problem: I want to manage or cancel my subscription
### Fix
1. Open **Settings**.
2. Tap **Manage subscription** if available.
3. Or tap **Cancel subscription**, which opens your app-store subscription settings.
4. You can also manage subscriptions directly in your Apple App Store account settings.

### Problem: I restored purchases but still do not have Pro
### Fix
1. Confirm the purchase was made using the same store account.
2. Confirm the subscription is still active and was not refunded, cancelled, or expired.
3. Reopen the app and check again.

---

## 7. Notifications

### Problem: I am not receiving notifications
### Fix
1. Make sure notifications are allowed for StayReel in your phone settings.
2. In StayReel, open **Settings** and verify your notification toggles are enabled.
3. If you recently changed notification permissions, close and reopen the app.
4. Take another snapshot and wait for the next eligible notification event.

### Problem: I turned notifications off and want them back
### Fix
1. Open your phone's Settings app.
2. Find StayReel.
3. Re-enable notifications.
4. Reopen StayReel.

---

## 8. Referral Codes and Profile Settings

### Problem: I skipped my referral code and want to add it later
### Fix
1. Open **Settings**.
2. Tap **Enter referral code** if that option appears.
3. Submit the code there.

### Problem: I want to change my school
### Fix
1. Open **Settings**.
2. Tap **School**.
3. Select your current school.

---

## 9. Privacy and Data Controls

### Problem: I want to disconnect Instagram without deleting everything
### Fix
1. Go to **Settings → Disconnect Instagram**.
2. This removes the active Instagram connection while keeping your StayReel account and saved history.

### Problem: I want all of my data deleted
### Fix
1. Go to **Settings → Delete all my data**.
2. Confirm the deletion.
3. This permanently removes your account data, snapshots, and related records.

### Problem: I only want to sign out
### Fix
1. Go to **Settings → Sign out**.
2. Signing out does not delete your saved data.

---

## 10. App Performance Problems

### Problem: The app feels stuck, blank, or slow
### Fix
1. Fully close and reopen StayReel.
2. Make sure your internet connection is stable.
3. Update to the latest app version.
4. Restart your phone if the issue continues.
5. Try again later if Instagram or app services may be temporarily slow.

### Problem: I hit an unexpected error
### Fix
1. Wait a few minutes.
2. Reopen the app.
3. Retry the action once.
4. If the issue keeps happening, gather details before contacting support:
   - what you tapped
   - the exact error message
   - whether the issue happened during sign-in, Instagram connection, snapshot capture, or purchase restore

---

## 11. What Usually Does *Not* Help

These steps usually do not fix account-related issues and can make troubleshooting harder:

- Repeatedly retrying snapshots during an Instagram rate limit
- Disconnecting and reconnecting Instagram multiple times in a row
- Buying Pro again instead of using **Restore Purchases**
- Deleting the app before checking whether the issue is just a temporary Instagram challenge or expired session
