🔐 How to connect Instagram

After confirming your account you'll connect your Instagram. Here's exactly what to do — a lot of beta users run into issues by skipping a step:

1
 Open Instagram in the StayReel browser
A built-in browser will open Instagram's login page. Do not use your phone's main Instagram app — you must log in inside StayReel.
2
 Sign in all the way to your home feed
Enter your username and password, complete any two-factor authentication, and wait until you can see your Instagram home feed. If you stop at the login screen, the connection will fail.
3
 Tap "Connect this account"
Once you're on your feed, tap the button at the bottom of the screen. StayReel will securely save your session — your password is never stored.

⚠️ Instagram alerts you might see

Instagram may send security alerts when StayReel reads your follower list. These are normal and expected. Here's what each one means:

🔔 "Suspicious Login Attempt"

Instagram detected an unusual sign-in. Open the Instagram app directly, tap the alert, and confirm it was you. StayReel will work again once you approve it.

🤖 "We Detected Automated Behavior"

Instagram flagged automated activity on your session. This can happen if snapshots run too close together. Our built-in delays (10–15 s between requests) are designed to minimize this. If you see it, wait an hour and try again — do not keep retrying immediately.

🛡 "Challenge Required" / Verify It's You

Instagram wants you to verify your identity via email, phone, or photo. Complete the challenge in the Instagram app, then go to StayReel Settings → Reconnect Instagram to refresh your session.

🔄 "Session Expired" in StayReel

Instagram sessions expire over time (usually every few weeks). Go to StayReel Settings → Reconnect Instagram and log in again. This is normal.

✅ Why does each snapshot take a few minutes?

We intentionally slow down requests (10–15 s between pages) and limit you to 3 snapshots per day to protect your account. This is by design, not a bug.
