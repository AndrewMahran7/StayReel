What is StayReel?

StayReel lets you take periodic snapshots of your Instagram followers and following lists so you can see who followed or unfollowed you over time. It is a read-only tool — it only views your public follower and following lists.

✅ What StayReel will never do

StayReel will never post, like, comment, follow, unfollow, or send messages on your behalf. It will never store your Instagram password. Your data is encrypted and never sold or shared.

⚠️ Seeing an Instagram security alert?

Instagram may occasionally show a security notice when any third-party tool reads your follower data. This is normal. Approve the alert in the Instagram app, then reconnect inside StayReel.

🤖 “Automated behavior” warning?

Wait at least 1 hour before trying again. Do not retry repeatedly — the warning clears on its own.

Need to reconnect Instagram?

If StayReel shows a session error, go to Settings → Reconnect Instagram and log all the way in to your home feed inside the app.
